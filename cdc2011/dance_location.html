All competition events will be held in the Lucas Ballroom at UNC Charlotte's Cone University Center. The Lucas Room is a banquet hall with audience-style seating max capacity for 300. The ballroom has acquired a new hardwood floor as January 2011. If you are planning to attend CDC 2011 we highly encourage you come tryout the floor before the comp. Check the Charlotte DanceSport calendar to find when we have the ballroom reserved.

Parking | Cone Deck

Park in the Cone Deck 2 which is adjacent to the Cone University Center. Parking is FREE during the weekend on campus, even at parking meters. If you find the Cone Deck to be full continue down University Road to Lot 5 or the West Desk.
