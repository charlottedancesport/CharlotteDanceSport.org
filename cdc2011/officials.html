CDC 2011 Officials

Jonathan Medlin
Master of Ceremonies

Jonathan Medlin and Malin Allert have been dancing together for about five years. They are active dancesport competitors, teachers, and choreographers. Jonathan is the former US Amateur American Ballroom National Champion. Jonathan has MCed this event for the last four years.

Maria St. John
Disc Jockey

Maria began her DJ career in college as an on-air DJ for 4 years. A member of the local ballroom community for over 13 years, she's been DJ'ing local dances, parties, competitive mini-matches, showcases, college and USA Dance-sanctioned competitions for over 8 years including the Charlotte Dancesport Challenge, the USC Gamecock Dancesport Challenge, the Carolina Fall Classic Dancesport Championships and the NCSU Triangle Open. She is very excited to once again be part of UNCC's fantastic event.

Marie Crowder
Adjudicator - NC

Marie is a four time USA Dance Amateur Champion in Latin and a 3-time USA representative to the IDSF World Latin Championships which were held in Belgium, Spain and Italy. Marie has won numerous Regional Standard and Latin Championship titles and was a Standard finalist in the USA DanceSport National Championships.

Marie is a proud member of the Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing (ISTD), with the following degrees:

  • Associate International Standard
  • Licentiate International Latin
  • Licentiate American Smooth
  • Licentiate American Rhythm

Marie also serves as Membership Services Director of the United States Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing and is a Regional Coordinator for USA Dance.

Wayne Crowder
Adjudicator - NC

Wayne is a four time USA Dance Amateur Champion in Latin and a 3-time USA representative to the IDSF World Latin Championships which were held in Belgium, Spain and Italy. Wayne has won numerous Regional Standard and Latin Championship titles and was a Standard finalist in the USA DanceSport National Championships.

Wayne is a Championship Adjudicator and a proud member of the Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing (ISTD), with the following degrees:

  • Licentiate International Standard
  • Licentiate International Latin
  • Licentiate American Smooth
  • Licentiate American Rhythm

Wayne also serves on the National Committee of the United States Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing, as well as a Dancesport Council member of USA Dance.

Clément Joly
Adjudicator - NC

Clément, co-owner, director and premier instructor, has been dancing since age thirteen. He is an accomplished Latin dance champion and performer. He is both a former Canadian Amateur Latin champion and a North American Professional Latin finalist. He has trained with internationally renowned instructors, including spending numerous years training, living, and competing in London, England. He has competed nationally and internationally and has represented Canada in France, Croatia, Mexico, Great Britain, Canada and United States. As a skilled dance performer, Clément has also appeared in the movies "The Aviator" and "The Courage to Love". In Charlotte, NC, Clément continues to compete and to coach amateur and Pro-Am students. Clément currently instructs at Metropolitan Ballroom in Charlotte, NC.

Anik Laforest
Adjudicator - NC

Anik Laforest is an accomplished Latin dance champion and performer. She is both a former Canadian Amateur Latin champion and a North American Latin finalist. She has competed and performed in France, Croatia, Mexico, Canada and the United States. As a skilled dance performer, she has also appeared in the movies "The Aviator" and "The Courage to Love". Anik enhanced her professional training by spending 2000 in England, and is certified by the Imperial Society of Teachers of Dancing (ISTD) in:

  • International Standard
  • International Latin
  • Theatre Arts

She is a member in good standing of the National Dance Council of America (NDCA), and has called Charlotte, North Carolina home since 2004. Anik currently instructs at Metropolitan Ballroom in Charlotte, NC. Contact info: 704-231-0284

Robertas Maleckis &
Inga Sirkaite
Adjudicators - NC

Robertas and Inga have been dancing together for 17 years. They started dancing in Lithuania and have danced in England for 2 years before moving to US. They won many prestigious titles throughout their successful dancing career. Robertas and Inga are currently residing in Durham, NC and own Elite Ballroom in Morrisville, NC.

Dimitrijus Sazinas
Adjudicator - NC

Dimitrijus Sazinas, born and raised in Lithuania, was exposed to dance in early years. Once in America, he continued to pursue his passion for dance, and currently competes at the top Professional level, Nationally and Internationally, in the Open International Standard Division. He has won several competitions and is a finalist in some the largest competitions throughout the nation. He is currently second in the nation and 8th in the world in the Classical Showdance Division. Dimitrijus is also the owner of Piper Glen Ballroom one of the leading Ballrooms in the South East.

Erica Sazinas
Adjudicator - NC

Erica Sazinas, born in Argentina, began to dance in her youth. Her inspiration came from her native country and obvious home of the Argentine Tango. She began intense training with world renowned coaches and choreographers, and she currently competes at the top Professional level, Nationally and Internationally in the Open Professional international Standard Division. She has won several competitions throughout the nation and is currently ranked second in the Nation and 8th in the world in the Professional Classical Showdance Division.

Eduardo Torres &
Cristina Acevedo
Adjudicators - SC

Eduardo and Cristina are originally from Puerto Rico. They grew up feeling the latin rhythms and discovered the world of ballroom in 1998. They started competing as amateurs becoming American Rhythm 2005 subchampions in USA Dance Nationals, and later decided to take it to the next level and became professionals. They firmly believe in showing emotion and auhtenticity through movement and love to share their passion for dancing with everyone. Some of their most recent accomplishments include:

  • 2010 Ohio Star Ball RS Rhythm finalist
  • 2010 Heritage RS Rhythm finalist
  • 2010 Southeastern RS Rhythm champions
  • 2010 Florida Superstar subchampions
  • 2010 Nashville Starz RS Rhythm subchampions

Sami & Karita Yli-Piipari
Adjudicators

Sami and Karita started to dance at the age of 10 in their native country Finland. During their career they achieved numerous international and national titles including 15 Finnish National Championships titles. They also were World & European 10 Dance Finalists. They are teachers with busy schedule and they have taught world best couples in more than 10 countries around the World. Sami was Finnish National Team Coach for four years across 2006 to 2009. They are qualified adjudicators and they have adjudicated several World and Continental Championships.
